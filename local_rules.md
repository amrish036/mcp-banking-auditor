# AML Compliance Policy (AUSTRAC Simulation)

## 1. High-Value Thresholds

- **Rule 101**: Any single transaction exceeding $10,000 AUD must be flagged for manual review.

## 2. Structuring & Smurfing

- **Rule 202**: Identify "Structuring" patterns where a single account makes multiple transactions on the same day that total more than $10,000 AUD, even if individual amounts are smaller.

## 3. High-Risk Descriptions

- **Rule 303**: Flag transactions with descriptions containing "Consulting", "Private Sale", or "Investment" if the account holder is not a verified business entity.
